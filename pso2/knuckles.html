Knuckle Normals

To get the most out of your PA rotations, you’ll need to understand how your normal attacks interact with your rotations, as doing so will enable you to be more PP efficient and have higher up time.
Knuckles have 3 attacks that regenerate PP:

Normal 1: 5 PP
Normal 2: 7 PP
Normal 3: 13 PP

Each normal or PA advances the combo by one action. Keeping track of these actions in a series of normal attacks is crucial to keeping damage and PP optimized during a rotation. In general, Normal 3 is the highest damage and most PP efficient of a Normal series. As such, Normal 3 can be weaved into a combo like as follows:

From Neutral: PA → PA → Normal 3 → PA → PA → Normal 3
From Neutral: Normal 1 → PA → PA → PA → PA → PA → Normal 3

Using Step or Step Attack will return the player to neutral, allowing you to reset your combo if needed. Additionally, Step or Step Attack will hold TAJA while you have Knuckle Gear

Photon Arts

Backhand Smash (40 PP)
(ONE PUNCH! But, more like 20 times.)

Knuckles' core bossing PA. A hard hitting,quick animation with extremely limited range. Useful against slow moving or knocked down enemies. Has VERY HIGH KNOCKBACK and sends small enemies FLYING.

Flash Thousand (20 PP)
(ORA ORA ORA ORA ORA ORA!)

Useful against enemies that Backhand Smash isn't cut out for, such as mobile targets or enemies with weird hitboxes.

Maintains altitude and has FRONTAL guard frames during the uppercut

Pendulumn Roll (30 PP)
(Go watch Hajime No Ippo)

Useful against enemies that Backhand Smash isn't cut out for, such as mobile targets or enemies with weird hitboxes.

Maintains altitude and has FRONTAL guard frames during the uppercut

Quake Howling (15 PP)
(Earthquake the TM)

Knuckles' core mobbing PA. A PP efficient PA that stuns mobs. Has super armor for its full duration.
Gains a larger AoE and slight tracking when used mid-air.

Slide Upper (20 PP) (Uncharged/Uncrafted)
(Just use Type-0 instead)

The uncharged variant of this PA should be used when you don't have enough time to charge it. Sometimes used against flimsy mobs.

Slide Upper Type-0 (10~20 PP)
(The bargain at the supermarket is today!)

A chargeable PA that's primarily used as a preemptive attack.

Should be charged and ready to release while waiting for an enemy to spawn or come towards you.

Maintains height if you cancel out of it towards the end of the PA. Great for retaining DPS in downtime and keeping Knuckle Gear up if you're aiming for an airborne mob.

Flicker Jab (10 PP)
(Consecutive normal punches)

Great as a setup for Backhand Smash due to its quick speed and low PP cost. Also used as fodder for Tech Arts Count Bonus.

Ducking Blow (8 PP)
(Nice try, big guy)

Extremely PP efficient PA that's mainly used as a defensive alternative to Flicker Jab. Also useful against aggressive humanoid enemies as it's basically a better sway.

Heartless Impact (15 PP)
(No, not Genshin Impact)

Great as a travel PA or gap closer. Has guard frames on startup. Dosen't lose elevation when used mid-air.

Suprise Knuckle (15PP)
(Suprise! It's not one, but 3 fists!)

A multi-purpose PA often used when Backhand Smash isn't viable. Very quick PA; while grounded, provider Super Armor and moves you towards your target.

Straight Charge (Uncrafted) (20 PP)
(Don't use this.)

Uncrafted, Straight Charge is straight garbage.

Straight Charge Type-0 (15~25 PP)
(THIS IS THE FIST THAT WILL PIERCE THE HEAVENS!)

Used to pursue moving targets. Deals continuous damage that increases to its maximum after six hits. Mainly used for keeping up with targets that are moving.
