Skill Trees




Since I'm not insane, I won’t bother you with every single detail of this mess of a tree. Instead, we’re only going to go over the important skills for Fighter, and its subclasses of choice, Hunter and Etoile. Also, if you’re asking “What about the other classes, Lux?” then here's my response…

These two subclasses are the best at the moment, as they both provide high damage and survivability that other subclasses can’t provide. Hunter and Etoile have a few differences in how they provide damage and survivability, which will be expanded on below. (Also, as of september 16th luster has joined the roster of subclasses.)

(Additionally, make sure to take the “High Level Bonus” skills on all main and subclass trees.)




General Fighter Skills

  • Critical Strike

    Increases your critical hit rate by 30% and increases your critical hit damage by 1.15x. Because of this skill’s huge damage boost, critical hit rate becomes fundamental to gearing up Fighter.

  • Knuckle Gear

    Increases the attack and animation speed for your Knuckle PAs proportional to your current level of gear, up to a max of level 3. Boosts the damage of some PAs at gear level 3. PAs and normals can contribute to gaining gear, while your weapon action can maintain, but not gain gear.

  • Knuckle Gear Boost

    Doubles the amount of gear obtained, so you only need 2 attacks to max it out instead of 3.

  • Twin Dagger Gear

    Twin Dagger gear increases the damage of Twin Dagger PAs while you’re in the air. You can only build gear in the air, and landing will cause you to lose all gear. Level 2 gear is a 1.2x damage increase while level 3 gear is a 1.5x damage increase.

  • Twin Dagger Spin Move

    Using Twin Daggers’ weapon action will perform a horizontal spin that can be JA’d to gain vertical height. During the spin animation you’re granted guard frames from all sides. You don’t have to learn this skill to use Twin Daggers’ weapon action, this skill just allows you to move during Twin Daggers’ weapon action.

  • Double Saber Gear

    Dealing damage fills up your gear. At level 1 DS gear, you can use your weapon action to summon a tornado. Your tornado persists around your character and does damage to nearby enemies. You may have two tornadoes active at once. Tornadoes should only be activated with the L/DS Kamaitachi ring, as that will provide tornadoes with TAJAs damage increase.

  • Double Saber Wind Parry

    Provides guard frames during DS’s weapon action. Needs level 1 of gear to activate. This skill has a short animation, so it’s a bit tricky to work with. Carries over TAJA benefits.

  • Brave Stance

    Increases damage dealt by 1.4x when attacking an enemy from the front. Only increases damage by 1.35x when attacking from the rear. This stance is your main damage multiplier, and the stance you’re going to be using most of the time. Simply put, punch enemies in the face and not in the back. This stance is activated by default when learned.

  • Brave Stance Up

    Further increases damage when attacking from the front by an extra 1.1x for a total multiplier of 1.54x.

  • Brave Critical

    Increases critical hit rate by 25% when attacking enemies from the front. Another simple skill, punch enemies in the face for more critical hit rate, and as mentioned before, you get more damage for landing critical hits. Note that critical hit rate is additive instead of multiplicative.

  • Wise Stance

    Increases damage dealt by 1.35x when attacking an enemy from the rear. Only increases damage by 1.3x when attacking from the front.

    This stance is secondary to Brave Stance, as you’ll usually be attacking enemies from the front. This stance is not passive and requires you to activate it from your subpalette. Activating Wise Stance will automatically deactivate Brave Stance. Normally, skill trees won’t put any skill points into this unless you’re specifically building a Wise Stance tree.

  • Wise Stance Up

    Increases damage dealt from the rear by an additional 1.3x for a total multiplier of 1.75x.

  • Wise Critical

    Increases critical hit rate by 25% when attacking the enemy from the rear. Just like Brave Critical, more critical hit rate means more damage, only this critical hit rate increase only applies when attacking from the rear. Again, increases to critical hit rate are additive, not multiplicative.

  • Adrenaline

    Increases the duration of each Shifta and Deband tick you receive by 30 seconds per tick. Normally, each tick of Shifta or Deband will only grant a 15 second buff. With this skill, it becomes 45 seconds per tick. Caps out at 60 seconds unless a Techer with Extend Assist is buffing you, in which case the cap is 180 seconds.

  • Limit Break

    On activation, you deal 1.2x extra damage for 60 seconds. Your max hp is cut down to 25% until Limit Break ends. This is the skill that makes Fighter fun and frightening, and it’s the skill that’ll lead you to tear your goddamn hair out in anger.

    A good chunk of your time as Fighter will be under LB and whenever it’s off cooldown, you should be activating it for synergy with your other skills. Limit break will activate Crazy Heart and Crazy Beat.

  • Limit Break Insurance

    If you take an attack that would kill you while Limit Break is active, Limit Break Insurance leaves you at 1 HP, and grants 5 seconds of invincibility. This will end LB prematurely, and increase the recast time of LB based on how many times you’ve triggered LBI.

    However, if you go a whole limit break duration without triggering this skill, your recast time for LB will reset back to it’s default cooldown. Iron Will triggers before LBI, but weapons that have a potential that save you (such as Atlas weapons) do not proc before LBI.

  • Limit Break Photon Charge

    When Limit Break ends, you recover all of your PP. This skill will not activate if you trigger LBI.

  • Halfline/Deadline Slayer

    When you drop to 50/25% of your HP, increase your ATK. This skill also comes into effect when you activate Limit Break. Heavy investment into slayer skills is not recommended as their attack increases are not further increased by team attack buff, Shifta drink, or the technique Shifta. If you have spare points, it’s better to invest in Fighter’s Striking Ups, which do scale with your buffs.

  • Crazy Beat

    When under a status abnormality, ie Burn, Poison, Injury, boost your S-ATK up to +200. Free damage is free damage, what can I say.

  • Crazy Heart

    When under a status abnormality, double your natural PP regeneration as well as PP gained by normal attacking. Crazy Heart is recommended because it makes Fighter PP management a lot easier.

  • Tech Arts JA Bonus

    Gain a 1.15x damage bonus when you JA with different PAs in succession. This skill defines the way you play Fighter, since it’ll require that you alternate PAs at all times.

    This skill’s damage bonus can be maintained while using normal attacks as long as you don’t drop your JAs, however normal attacks’ damage does not benefit from this skill. TAJA bonus can also be maintained through Step Attacks.

  • Tech Arts JA PP Save

    When under a status abnormality, double your natural PP regeneration as well as PP gained by normal attacking. Crazy Heart is recommended because it makes Fighter PP management a lot easier.




General Hunter Skills

  • JA Bonus 1 & 2

    Just Attacks in general increase your damage by 1.3x - these skills increase that up to 1.573x. Excellent synergy with Fighter skills as JAing is essential to proper Fighter gameplay

  • Fury Stance, Fury S Up 1 & 2

    This stance grants you an unconditional damage multiplier totaling up to 1.323x.

  • Fury Critical

    Increases your critical hit rate by 25%.

  • Fury Combo Up

    Every successive Just Attack grants you a stacking damage bonus, up to 1.1x. The percentages listed in this PAs description list how much damage you gain per Just Attack. Ie, 5/10 means you max out with 2 JAs while 10/10 maxes you out with only 1 JA.

  • Iron Will

    Grants a 75% chance to survive a killing blow. Grants 5 seconds of invincibility when this skill is activated. This skill is one Fighter’s favorite tools provided by Hunter sub, as LB leaves you low enough to get killed in one hit by many attacks. The major downside to this skill is that it’s RNG based.Do you feel lucky, punk?

  • Never Give Up

    Increases S-ATK by 300 for 60 seconds when you activate Iron Will. Increases the invincibility duration of Iron will to 15s. The only downside to this skill is that in order to proc it, you need to proc Iron Will first.

  • Massive Hunter

    Reduces damage taken by 25% and prevents you from being flinched or knocked down for 45 seconds. Another important skill to pick up as it allows you to stand your ground against anything that might give you trouble.




General Etoile Skills

  • Etoile HP Restorate

    Recovers 2.5% of your health every 3 seconds while below 50% HP. HP Restorate allows you to take hits and slowly recover your HP - take some hits, and keep on fighting without stopping to heal.This skill heals for less while Limit Break is active, as Limit Break reduces your max HP.

  • Et All Attack Bonus

    Gain a 1.35x increase. MORE FREE DAMAGE. Take this or you’re a nerd and a loser.

  • Flat Attack Bonus

    Increases your damage by 1.3x whenever you miss a JA. You’re a fighter. With TAJA, JA Bonus, and TAJA PP Save - try not to miss JAs too often. Invest a minimum of 4 SP into this cause you’re not a robot - you won’t land 100% of your JAs.

  • Standing Massive

    Prevents knockback and flinch while standing still. It’s basically a more limited version of Massive Hunter, but with a damage reduction. The same things that apply to Massive Hunter apply to this as well, but you need to stand still for this skill to take effect.

  • Damage Balancer

    Reduces damage taken by 70%, but also reduces all healing that doesn’t come from items or Etoile’s tree by 100%. Damage Balancer is your main survival skill, as it allows you to use Limit Break without getting squashed immediately. Only being able to heal through items and Etoile’s HP regen is a big loss though, and it affects how you set up your gear.

  • Etoile Boost

    Provides you with 15% damage reduction every time you get hit, up to a max of 75%. Decays by 25% per second. Very useful against multi-hit attacks, but won’t save you from anything that would instantly kill you. A useful skill for only 1 SP.

  • Same Arts PP Save

    Reduces the amount of PP consumed when a PA is used consecutively, up to an 80% reduction. This skill allows you to spam more PP consuming PAs. Now you can use Quake Howling and Backhand Smash over, and over, and over, and over… Note only invest 3/10 points into this as you ideally would want to repeat a PA 3x in a combo.

  • Tech Arts Count Bonus

    Grants a damage bonus of 1.15x and reduces PP consumption by 10% when you use different PAs in a combo. One PA may be repeated up to 3 times before this skill’s bonuses are lost. A better TAJA that allows you to get away with using your better PAs multiple times in a row. Also works with Same Arts PP Save.

  • Etoile JA Bonus, JA Crit

    When you JA, increase damage by 1.15x and your critical hit rate by 50%. Yet another reason to not drop your JAs.




General Luster Skills

Luster notes: Luster subclass adopts the voltage mechanic, which grants damage, critical, and damage resistance as your voltage increases. Benefits cap at 500 volts.

  • Luster Voltage

    Increases voltage based on the number of hits. Gain bonuses to damage up to 10% and damage reduction based on the voltage up to 20%.

  • Voltage Bonus

    Every time you gain 100 volts, you gain a tick of Shifta and Deband. (Won’t do hecking nothing if you don’t have Shifta or Deband learned you nerd.)

  • High Voltage

    Buff scaling up to 25% crit, 150% passive and active pp regen at 500 volts, and at 500 gain 50% cooldown reduction.

  • Voltage Reset Heal

    Upon losing your voltage you restore hp and pp based on the voltage lost.

  • Voltage Heat Up

    You get one extra voltage on hitting a boss/mini boss. Example Dark Falz Persona, Rockbear gets one extra volt added.

  • Tech Arts JA Multi Bonus

    Just attacking using PAs in a combo boosts voltage gain by +1 and grants you 25% critical rate.

  • Tech Arts PP High Save

    Reduces PP consumption in combos of 4 or more distinct PAs and Techs, taking effect on the 3rd.

  • All Attack Bonus Lu

    Gain 170% extra striking damage. Take this or you’re just a hecking loser AND nerd.

  • Killing Restorate

    Recovers HP and PP on enemy defeat. The range is about 2 steps.

  • Amplified Assist

    Boosts each tick of Shifta and Deband granted on yourself, and breaks the cap of 60 seconds to 180 seconds.

  • Deband Disorder

    while under Deband, boosts your chance to inflict status effects. This skill is very good if you’re looking at chase builds.

  • Shifta Gauge Boost

    While under Shifta, boosts gear and photon blast generation rates.

  • Complete Rest

    More like complete trash because standing still in 2020 lmao. If for some reason you want to waste points in this skill only take one please cool thx.




Skill Trees For Knuckleheads



General tree: Standard Fi/Hu build.

Wise Stance: A Fi/Hu tree that runs Wise Stance instead of Brave Stance. Useful in content where Wise Stance's bonuses are consistently active, such as Dark Falz Elder.

Chase tree: A Fi/Hu tree that takes advantage of Double Saber's rapid hits and Quake Howling by using S7: Zenith Impact to easily inflict status effects. Capitalizes on this with Chase Bind and increased investment in Chase Advance.

Et sub: A Fighter tree that focuses on Knuckles and Twin Daggers. Provides weaker Double Saber damage if you want to use it as well, but improved PP usage via the Etoile skills Tech Arts Count Bonus and Same Arts PP Save.

Luster sub: Eschews most defensive tools for increased damage, featuring 100% uptime on Limit Break at 500 Voltage.
