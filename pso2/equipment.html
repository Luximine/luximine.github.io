Recommended 15★ Weapons

  • 15★ Lightweaver Cras Knocker

    Potential: It’s Darkbanisher’s potential upgraded. After using 3 normal attacks, for 30 seconds it changes the potential to: 18.72% damage reduces damage taken by 30% and negates knockback, then goes on cd for 30 seconds after the effect ends. SSA support: S1~S5.

    Upgraded Darkbanisher with better stats and evolved potential. With it’s wide array of ssa slots Lightweaver and the upgraded potential makes itself one of the best weapons you can pick up for yourself.

  • 15★ Darkbanisher Ayer Knocker

    Potential: 12% Damage, reduce pp consumption by 20%, recovers 20 pp every 10 seconds. SSA support: S1~S4. SATK 2754

    Another great pickup when you get it and it just gets even better when you upgrade it into its final form. Currently as just a darkbanisher it’s better than puras for just an offhand weapon, worse than stil for a mainhand weapon.

  • 15★ Fluxio Vorona

    Potential: 13% Damage. When you hit with an attack: boost damage by an additional 8%,reduce damage taken by 20% and boost your max pp by 100 for 20 seconds. Upon dealing 1,500,000 damage the effect is regenerated. SSA support S1, S4~S5. SATK 2821

    Fluxio Voroana is a rather interesting weapon to Fighter’s arsenal while missing s2-s3 support hurts, it’s potential almost makes it up just being 4% under Cras. This weapon really shines with S4: Radiant Response, as the extra pp you get just for dealing damage boosts it’s pp efficiency with it on.

    Note: currently only available as title for just clearing CM3 and defeating Omega Hunar.

  • 15★ Forza Stil

    Potential: Power bonus decreases when taking damage (3% damage lost per hit with a min damage bonus of 10%) and recovers over time (0.4% per sec; takes 15 seconds to fully stack up). When power bonus is 16%, gains super armor, increases natural PP recovery by 60%, regenerates 20 pp every second, and gives 40% damage resistance. SSA Support: S1~3, S5. SATK: 2731

    Upgraded Puras with much better stats and an upgraded potential. Alright weapon at the moment with a high damage potential and its ability to weaken a blow that might kill you and a wide range of SSA’s to use.

  • 15★ Forza Puras

    Potential: Power bonus decreases when taking damage (3% damage lost per hit with a minimum damage bonus of 10%) and recovers over time (0.4% per sec; takes 15 seconds to fully stack up). When power bonus is 16% gains super armor and 40% damage resistance. SSA Support: S1~3, S5. SATK: 2377

    A great pickup when you eventually get all of the other mats needed to make Forza. Even at the minimum potential damage, Puras is still a strong weapon that can hold its own vs the other 15 stars.

  • 15★ Liberate Knuckles

    Potential+: 23% Damage, increases critical rate by 20% and decreases PP consumption by 15%. SSA Support: S4~S5, SATK: 2606

    While it doesn't have S1-3 support, the weapon’s raw stats, special S5’s available, and the extra 20% crit allows Fighter to reach 100% crit to make full use of Fighter’s Critical Strike skill and the added pp consumption is a nice cherry on top.

  • 15★ Novel Knuckles

    Potential: 14% Damage, increases Critical Hit Rate by 20%. SSA Support: S4. SATK: 2317

    Easy to pick up and an ok weapon to pick up until you upgrade it to Liberate, doesn’t have S1-3 support but has a 14% passive damage boost to make up

  • 15★ Lightstream Asserxion

    Potential+: 17% Damage, fully restores PP when below 20% every 2 minutes and gains an additional 3% damage for 30 seconds when this occurs(120 recast cd). SSA Support: S1~3. SATK: 2393

    To make full use of this weapon’s S1 SAF, a +200 atk affix is required as this will provide a 5% boost which is the highest amongst all SSAs a Fi would use. If you want to make Lightstream your main weapon, it’s important to note the lack of healing due to the lack of S4. I don’t recommend S2: Sturdy Recovery on LS because giving up a slot that could be used for damage makes it slightly weaker than Altas Ex

  • 15★ Atlas Body Ex+

    Potential+: 18% Damage, Survive one fatal attack and restore HP at intervals after. SSA Support: S1~4. SATK: 2311

    Baseline 15 star. Atlas Ex has good damage output while giving you the option to take S4: Vampiric Strike or S4:Colossal Radiance for life steal or PP regen. Although Liberate outright beats this weapon, it’s still a great second pick if you don’t have Liberate ready.

  • 15★ Austere Fists-NT

    Potential+: Increase Natural PP Recovery by 10% SSA Support: S1~3. SATK: 2205

    Phantasmal Darkness: Increases power based on PB gauge (18%/19%/20%), receive additional effects with the release of various PB types

    Helix: Increase Power by 6%
    Ajax: Increase Active PP Recovery by 60%
    Ketos: Increase Natural PP Recovery by 40%
    Julius: Decrease damage taken by 15%
    Ilios: Reduce PP consumption by 20%
    Darkness Shield: 9% Damage, Generate a barrier after 30s giving -25% pp cost reduction
    Darkness Ward: 9% damage, Generate a barrier after 30s giving; Negate knockback effect, 30% pp automatic PP recovery, 40% PP recovery from attacks.

    As an actual weapon, it’s not really comparable to the other 15 stars other than endless, but as a PP battery, it’s the best around. With its Phantasmal Darkness potential and a Ketos Photon Blast, you gain the Natural PP Recovery effect along with Austere’s baseline 10%, it beats out any other pp battery you can get your hands on.

  • 15★ Shiho Genbu

    Potential: Increase Power by 8%. Reduce damage taken by 20% and gain knockback resistance. SSA Support: S3. SATK: 2351

    Shiho Genbu is an alright weapon (if you don’t have anything else) as Fighter/Etoile because it makes you really unkillable with Damage Balancer and Etoile Boost, while the downside about it is that its 1.08x damage is rather lackluster.

Recommended Left Rings

  • L/TD Air Chase

    Normal attacks and some PAs will follow the locked on enemy while you're airborne. Useful for tracking and keeping up with enemies when you need to pull out your Twin Daggers.

  • L/DS Kamaitachi

    Allows for a Whirlwind to proc with PAs
    Haha Tornado go WOOSH
    More info about this in FiHu DS Guide

  • L/Knuckle Chase

    Allows you to chase locked on enemies with certain PAs and normal attacks while on the ground. Same thing with Air Chase, useful for tracking and keeping up with your enemies when they want to move around.

  • L/Slow Fi Action

    Decrease the Speed of your Weapon Action with Fighter Weapons. This mostly helps for Knuckle Sway for Gear Experience counters as it’s hard to pull off because of how naturally fast the sway is.

  • L/Atomizer Lovers

    Increases the amount restored and speeds up the animation speed of Atomizer items, while giving you iframes during it.
    Heal and or pick up your teammates (or dead weight) without putting yourself at risk of getting interrupted or killed. Gotta get my 20 fun, :)

  • L/Jumping Dodge

    Enables Jumping Dodge to be set on the subpalette, which, when activated, will jump twice as high as normal with some iframes on startup. Extremely useful!




Recommended Right Rings

  • R/Effort Symbol

    An additional 1% damage multiplier , -3% damage taken, +10% crit chance. The extra damage is ok,-3% to damage taking is a nice treat as well but the 10% extra crit is really why you want this tbh.

  • R/P Keeper Strike

    Grants 3% extra damage while above 75% hp. Useful if you have a Techer in your mpa or party as the Deband will cover you for the -3% damage taken and the Shifta will grant you extra crit as well.

  • R/PP Convert

    For a period of time, reduce your maximum HP to increase natural PP regeneration rate. Useful to keep your pp up in fights and out of fights.




SSA’s To Think About

  • S1: Augment Intent 2 - Increase Power by 0.75% for each Special Ability attached to this weapon. Maxes at 6%.

  • S1: Augment Intent - Increases Power by 0.5% for each Special Ability attached to this weapon. Maxes at 4%.

  • S1: Skilled Intent - Increases Critical Hit Damage by 3%. extra crit damage is always nice

  • S1: Strike Boost - Increases Power by 1% for every 60 S-ATK affixed onto your weapon and 5% when at least 200 S-ATK is affixed. Only go for this if you can affix 200 S-atk to your weapon.

  • S2: Skilled Intent 2 - Increases Critical Hit Damage by 4%. Highest damage providing SSA in the S2 slots.

  • S2: Skilled Intent - Increases Critical Hit Damage by 3%. Same as the S1 and overall works well if you don’t have enough funds for SS2.

  • S2: Offensive Intent - Increases damage by 2%. If somehow you can’t get your hands on the other Skilled Strikes, this can work as well.

  • S2: Photon Reduction 2 - PP consumption is reduced by 13%. Only to be used if no other damage SSAs are available or if PP pool is really small.

  • S3: Skilled Intent 2 - Increases Critical Hit Damage by 5%. *Same thing for S2 SS2*

  • S3: Skilled Intent - Increases Critical Hit Damage by 4%. *Same thing for S2 SS*

  • S3: Vital Intent - Boosts damage by 3% while HP is 80% or more. *Same thing for Offensive Intent*

  • S3: Photon Reduction - PP consumption is reduced by 7%. *Same thing for Photon Reduction 2*

  • S3: Powering Intent - Increases damage dealt to bosses by 4%. If you intend on using knuckles as a pure bossing weapon then go right ahead.

  • S4: Vampiric Strike - Restores HP equal to 1% of damage dealt (30 HP MAX). Can't go wrong with lifesteal to keep you going.

  • S4: Raising Pursuit - Upon hitting with a PA/Technique, tracks 30% of damage dealt within the next four seconds, and adds additional damage to the next PA/Technique based on the damage dealt. (250,000 Max Damage) (10s Cooldown)

  • S4: Skilled Balance - Reduces Critical Hit Rate by 50% and increases Critical Hit damage by 6% *only useable with Liberate weapons with Mysterious Intent*

  • S4: Colossal Radiance - Recovers 40 PP every 10 seconds when there is a boss nearby. For PP upkeep in bossing content or big raid EQs.

  • S5: Evasive Meteor - Successfully evade an attack with Sway to summon a giant fist. Increases Power by 2%. Knuckles only. Gear Experience 2.0

  • S5: Swift Radiance - Stop Natural PP Regeneration. Increase Active PP Recovery by 50% and restore 100 PP upon successfully evading an attack with Sway or Step. Increase Power by 2%. Knuckles only. Running low on pp can’t be me.

  • S5: Mysterious Purpose - ncreases Critical Hit Rate by 20%. On a Critical Hit, deals an additional 2% damage and recovers 1% of damage dealt as HP (30 MAX). Vampstrike 2.0

  • S5: Antithesis Cultivation - Doubles the effects of Special Abilities (Atk, Def, Dex, HP, PP bonuses only) *Has the most effect if you’re going all out on your affixes*

  • S5: Tethered Eclipse - Boosts damage by 3% against enemies inflicted with a status effect. Grants a 40% chance to inflict Bind against enemies inflicted with a status effect. This SSA is meant for a Chase mobbing build that I won’t be going over but it’s notable anyhow.

  • S6: Overlimit - Increases maximum HP by 50% while Limit Break is active. While having an increased hp, Limit break will still take 25% of your new maximum hp. *effectively 37.5% of your previous maximum HP*

  • S6: Ironclad Illness - Become massive for 60 seconds after becoming affected by a status effect. Ring massive hunter every time you pop LB.

  • S6: S-ATK UP - Increases S-ATK by 30.

  • S6: Wise Skill - Increases your Critical Hit Rate by 15%. Overcaps crit by 5%

  • S7: S-ATK Up 2 - Increases S-ATK by 50.

  • S7: ATK UP 2 - Increases ATK By 45.

  • S7:Descried Warrior - Boosts damage against enemies targeting you by 1% and reduces damage taken from them by 15%. This S7 is mainly for solo content such as endless and solo triggers

  • S7: Zenith Impact - Gains a 10% chance to stun the enemy with a Just Attack. Used primarily for Chase builds.

  • S8: S-ATK UP - Increases S-ATK by 30.

  • S8: HP UP - Increases HP by 40.

  • S8: Sky Dance Boon - Activates Shifta/Deband every 10 seconds when you stay in the air for longer than 2 seconds. The best S8 out there if you’re lucky enough to drop this.

Other Info

Picking up a Helen Bathina (all class katana) is highly recommended as your overall mobility and your own travel methods aren’t really great for LONG distance travels long wherever you’re going is more than 2 heartless impacts away, as picking up a helen bathina and using Asagiri-rendan as it’s a faster pa and less pp than using heartless impact

Picking up something that’ll allow you to cast Shifta on yourself like Selc Flotz, Blood React (all class Jet boots with access to strike gust for Shifta), or Eclucio Culte (all class Talis with access to Shifta and Deband) or Sky Dance Boon. Having access to Shifta yourself is a nice addition to have when all you need is just 2 ticks of Shifta or Deband to max yourself out, and or just to keep a Techer’s Shifta going on you as well.

Since your mag will be pure S-ATK you would want to be feeding it any S-ATK weapons (weapons with red background for S-ATK), and to balance the DEX that rise you should also feed it 4★ Furniture, and or Monomates. (Note that mates items lower less DEX than Furniture.)

And the final note as playing Fighter LB is your friend and your greatest enemy that you’ll have to conquer in order to truly master, you will die a lot getting to master this class but that’s ok know your limits and slowly and surely break them, and I'm sure you’ll be just fine in your time playing Fighter.
